Chilli Prawn Spaghetti

Prep-Time: 10 mins


Cook-Time: 20 mins

Servings: 2



Ingredients:
















Steps:



  1. Cook pasta in a large saucepan of boiling, salted water following packet directions, until tender, lastly drain, reserving 1/4 cup cooking liquid.


  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat, then add garlic and chilli. Cook for 1 minute or until fragrant, then add prawns.

    Cook, stirring, for 2 to 3 minutes or until pink and cooked through, then add pasta, tomato and cooking liquid, then cook for 2 minutes or until heated through, then Season with salt and pepper, lastly Stir through parsley and serve.
