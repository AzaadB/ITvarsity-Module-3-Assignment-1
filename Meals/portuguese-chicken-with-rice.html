Portuguese Chicken with Rice

Prep-Time: 12 mins


Cook-Time: 12 mins

Servings: 4



Ingredients:
























Steps:



  1. Combine chicken, cumin, mustard powder, sambal oelek or chilli sauce, oil and half the garlic in a large bowl.

    Heat a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
    Cook chicken, in 2 batches, for 3 mins each side or until cooked through.

    Transfer to a plate and cover with foil to keep warm.


  2. Reduce heat to medium, then add the corn to the pan. Cook, stirring, for 3 mins or until golden, then add capsicum and rice. Transfer to a serving bowl.


  3. Whisk the yoghurt, mint and remaining garlic in a bowl and season.


  4. Arrange chicken on a serving platter. Drizzle with the yoghurt mixture. Serve with the rice mixture.
